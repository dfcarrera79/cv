Diego Fernando Carrera

Telecommunications Engineering, Ph.D. ResearchGate

I received a B.Sc. in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering from Universidad Técnica Particular de Loja (UTPL), Ecuador in 2012, an M.Sc. in Information Technology Management in 2015, and a Ph.D. in Telecommunications Engineering in 2020 from Tecnologico de Monterrey, Mexico. My research interests include statistical signal processing and machine learning techniques for wireless communication systems, channel estimation, detection, 5G/6G, mmWave, and massive MIMO systems.
